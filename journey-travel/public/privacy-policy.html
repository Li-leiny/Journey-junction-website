Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Journey Junction. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Information We Collect

  • Your personal details, such as name and email address, when you create an account or make a booking.
  • Geographic location for travel recommendations in Kenya.
  • Device and browsing data for website optimization and analytics purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate your travel bookings and provide personalized services.
  • To improve your user experience by tailoring content and recommendations.
  • To ensure compliance with Kenyan regulations related to data protection.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by Kenyan law.
  • To trusted partners for travel arrangements, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@journeyjunction.com.
